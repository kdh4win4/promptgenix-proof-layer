PromptGenix Proof Layer

Immutable AI Output & Data Provenance

The Problem

AI-generated content can be modified after creation, lacks reliable provenance, and is difficult to audit. This undermines trust in research, compliance, and decision-making workflows.

The Solution

PromptGenix Proof Layer provides an immutable proof mechanism for AI outputs by hashing prompts and results, attaching structured metadata, and storing proofs permanently on Arweave. Verification is independent, lightweight, and does not expose sensitive data.

Key Features

  • Cryptographic hashing of AI prompts and outputs
  • Permanent decentralized storage (Arweave)
  • Independent verification via transaction ID
  • Privacy-preserving by design

Use Cases

  • Scientific and regulatory reporting
  • Government and defense analytics
  • Legal and compliance documentation
  • Enterprise AI governance

Why It Matters

As AI adoption accelerates, trust infrastructure becomes critical. PromptGenix Proof Layer establishes a foundational capability for verifiable, auditable, and reproducible AI-assisted decision-making.
